Message from the Director

“Children are like buds in a garden and should be carefully and lovingly nurtured, as they are the future of the nation and the citizens of tomorrow.”

Pandit Jawahar Lal Nehru

A small step towards school for a vivid exposure in a child's life……

Children are like wet clay which are to be molded into beguiling figurines. We at Rainbow Kids aim at the integral development, characterized by dose and careful monitoring of each child and their development; always keeping in mind their individual process of growth. This is an exciting learning state where a child's natural curiosity and spontaneity will be given a center stage of expression. Each child will encounter the creative spaces and allow for exploration and discovery and that will activate their imagination.

The main tenet of our school is the acquisition of grail that will open their windows to the world and accept it with hope and respect for the social and natural gifts ensured by our teachers and mentors who aim to provide the knowledge along with love and care. We aspire to develop self-esteem, good personal habits, basic social values, cognitive maturity through joyful and positive experiences that will allow the tender minds to be comprehensive and accumulative.

We will share our joy and concern to the kids in the journey of their growing and encourage them to develop their own abilities and qualities. Together we shall discover their flair that will reflect their uniqueness with distinctive capabilities.




Message from the Principal

As being a preschool entrepreneur it's an essential role where we aim to provide a complete interactive learning with creativity and constructivism. As placing foundation bricks in the preliminary age of a preschooler, we pose for the responsible duty to show our innovative approaches and genuine concern to make their early learning memories joyful and conceited.

It's necessary to work and enhance upon stimulating and infusing their individual instincts and abilities to ameliorate and reveal the glaze of their personality by nurturing their natural inquisitiveness.

Rainbow Kids ensures for the development of thinking through a strong curriculum which proposes the child’s capability to perform basic self care including physical, social and emotional competence afferent with development of communication skills along with intelligence through a holistic development procedure which will indulge and breed the positive strength and confidence to express them between the vast exposure where they will start for the further tenure of formal schooling.

Assenting to the thoughts of famous philosopher and physician Maria Montessori, which refers “ The goal of early childhood should be to captivate the child’s own natural desire to learn.”
