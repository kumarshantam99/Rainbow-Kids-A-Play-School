Join Us!

Play is an essential part of early learning. Children are not meant to sit still, but to play and grow. Being a part of our passionate crew will let your children become the best version of themselves. Our fun filled activities, theme concept learning and modern teaching aids aim at developing your children's cognitive, socio-emotional and physical skills.

Ages for Admission: 2 and 3 years onward.

Application Procedure:

  • Download the application form by selecting 'Pay Now' and paying the application form fees.
  • Fill out the application form attentively.
  • Submit the application form with required Identification proofs and a copy of payment confirmation email to the school office.
